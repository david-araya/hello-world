CUAIS 2020: Workshops, Talks, and Projects

Carleton University Artificial Intelligence Society

December 23, 2019

The Carleton Artificial Intelligence Society is a group of likeminded Artificial Intelligence enthusiasts that meet on a weekly basis to learn and discuss Artificial Intelligence as well as develop AI applications. We provide workshops, talks, and projects to enable students the skills necessary to tackle the AI workforce.

I am excited to tackle a new role, as the President of the Carleton Artificial Intelligence Society starting in the Winter of 2020. We are one of the fastest-growing clubs at Carleton University, and our goal has always been to help students succeed in the field of Artificial Intelligence.  As a result, we will be providing an abundance of new tools and services to support our expanding member base in the new term.
